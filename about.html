Reflection

Redesign process, design experience, and client feedback

Redesign process: I started by organizing the content into clear sections, then layered in the visual style so each part of the site felt intentional and calm. The final layout was built to be responsive first, so the design stays balanced on smaller screens too.

Design & experience: I wanted the interface to feel like an editorial garden journal rather than a standard template. The soft palette, serif headings, and AI-style illustrations make the experience feel polished without becoming busy.

Client feedback: Feedback from the imagined client direction focused on keeping the site soothing, environmentally aware, and visually memorable. That led to the seasonal feature, testimonials, and an About page that keeps the storytelling consistent.
